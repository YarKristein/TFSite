Four tools that turn your phone into the only thing you need in your jersey pocket. Discover the line, lay it down, plan the send, find your crew.

01 — Discover
Find the
good dirt.

Browse official and community-imported routes near you. Filter by difficulty, distance, and elevation — tap any trail to see what you're in for.

  • Filter byDifficulty · Distance · Elevation · Surface
  • Map layersApple Maps · Hybrid · Satellite
  • GPXImport your own. Share official routes.
02 — Record
Lay down
your line.

GPS recording with live distance, time, and elevation. Big orange REC button because your hands are on the bars and your eyes are on the trail.

  • Live statsDistance · Time · Ascent
  • OutputGPX export · Share to crew
  • BatteryOptimized for all-day rides
03 — Group Rides
Plan the
send.

Pick a trail, pick a date, invite your crew. Meeting point, notes, group chat — everyone shows up to the right trailhead at the right time.

  • InviteFrom your contacts, by handle
  • ChatBuilt-in group chat per ride
  • MeetingPinned location, drop-in notes
04 — Radar
Stop riding
alone.

See other TrailFreakers nearby. Toggle sharing, ride up, make a new friend. Your crew was always out there — Radar just puts them on the map.

  • PrivacyOpt-in · off by default · one-tap toggle
  • Radius10, 25, or 50 km around you
  • ConnectFollow, invite to next ride
